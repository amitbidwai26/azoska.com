About Us

We founded Azoska in 2016 as an virtual business.Azoska focuses on driving business revenue for clients by providing B2B lead generation services.We are headquartered in Colombo, Sri Lanka,but works with national and international clients.
Azoska unique approach combines experience and knowledge not only in lead generation, but also in customer profiling, customer support services & CRM Implementation services and has allowed the company to experience impressive growth despite many challenges presented by the economy.


Market Coverage

Since qualifying leads is a process, many people take the easy route and move on to the next contact. Azoska, however, takes the time and effort to qualify each of your contacts, despite unavoidable numbers of missed calls, voicemails, unopened emails, or other missed communication methods. When prospects in your target market are left untouched, they will eventually go to one of your competitors. Azoska ensures that every prospect that meets your established definition of a qualified lead is sales-ready.


Market Intelligence

We approach every prospect not to merely rattle off a few questions, but to engage them in a real conversation. Azoska and our teams are trained to uncover market intelligence such as industry challenges, goals, priorities, industry trends, the competitive landscape, and more. Your sales team will be armed with insightful knowledge when they reach out to your qualified leads.

Lead Nurturing

In most B2B industries, not every contact is ready to purchase right away. This is why Azoska take the time to build and nurture relationships with all leads. As they display certain characteristics in the buying process, a trained Business Development Associate will be able to pinpoint where they are in the buying process. When that lead is ready to purchase, they will have your solution in mind because you helped educate them.

What are hot leads?

Hot leads are the leads that we contact and who respond with interest. The amount varies depending on your service, price, offer, and your target market but you will never receive less than the number that we guarantee on our plans.

What are qulity leads?

The quality leads are all the ideal companies that we're reaching out to, on your behalf. This list is very valuable, as all quality leads will match your ideal customer profile.
